About RK Banquet Hall

RK Banquet Hall, Ghaziabad is a great venue for weddings, pre-wedding ceremonies, and other social events. Conveniently located near the famous PK Charitable Hospital, it is easy for guests to find. The venue offers banquet halls perfect for small and mid-sized functions, along with a terrace for open-air events providing spectacular views at night.

The complimentary changing rooms ensure you look your best on your special day. RK Banquet Hall serves mouthwatering multi-cuisine delicacies in both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options. Alcohol can also be served to make your events livelier. With its elegant decor and warm ambiance, the venue is ideal for a grand celebration. The friendly staff ensures all guests have a pleasant experience while the management takes care of food and decor so you can focus on your day.
