About Planet Hollywood

Offering an outdoor swimming pool and a fitness centre, Planet Hollywood Beach Resort Goa is located on the stunning Uttorda Beach. This pet-friendly accommodation ensures world-class service with air-conditioned rooms, private balconies, and luxurious bathrooms complete with free toiletries and a hairdryer.

The property features a 24-hour front desk, a games room, a tour desk, and ample luggage storage facilities. Guests can enjoy free parking and indulge in delightful cuisines at the in-house restaurants: Fame, serving international delicacies, and Plant Dailies, a charming coffee shop.

Location

Planet Hollywood Beach Resort Goa, Uttorda Beach, Goa, India

Nearby attractions: Colva Beach (6 km), Benaulim Beach (8 km), Goa International Airport (7 km), Margaon Bus Station (9 km), and Madgaon Railway Station (12 km).
