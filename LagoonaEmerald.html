About Lagoona Emerald

Lagoona Emerald, Saket, Delhi, is the perfect venue for planning the wedding of your dreams. Known for its elegance and versatility, this venue is ideal for hosting pre-wedding rituals like haldi, sangeet, and mehndi. It is also well-suited for lavish weddings, receptions, and engagement ceremonies.

This venue offers a versatile space for social events, private gatherings, conferences, awards, exhibitions, product launches, building events, seminars, anniversaries, birthday parties, workshops, family get-togethers, and much more. With a range of options, it ensures a memorable experience for every occasion.

  • Hall/Lawn: Seating for 125, Max Capacity: 200
