Dharamshala Stadium

A Picturesque Venue Nestled in the Heart of the Himalayas

About Dharamshala Stadium

Earlier, plans were made by the BCCI and the state government to construct an international stadium in Shimla, with the proposed location being Annadale. However, the Army rejected the proposal, citing the strategic importance of Annadale as a cantonment area. Consequently, the plan was shifted to Dharamshala, where the HPCA Stadium was finally built.

The stadium serves as the home ground for the Himachal Pradesh cricket team for Ranji Trophy and other domestic matches. It also hosted IPL matches as a home stadium for Punjab Kings, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding Himalayan ranges.

Location

Dharamshala, Kangra, Himachal Pradesh, India

Home to: Himachal Pradesh cricket team and Himachal Pradesh women's cricket team.
